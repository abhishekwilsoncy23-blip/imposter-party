IMPOSTER

How to Play 📜

  • Add Players: Enter 3 or more names and set a time limit.
  • Pass the Phone: Everyone taps to secretly view their role. Most get a Secret Word, but one person is the Imposter!
  • Ask Questions: The "First Speaker" starts by asking someone else a vague question about the word. (e.g., "Is this found indoors?")
  • Don't get caught: The Imposter has to lie and blend in to avoid suspicion.
  • Vote: When the timer ends, vote on who the Imposter is!
